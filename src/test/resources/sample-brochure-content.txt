Investment Advisory Services

We provide comprehensive investment management services to institutional and individual clients.

We utilize Institutional Shareholder Services (ISS) for proxy research and voting recommendations.

Class action settlements are monitored through Robbins Geller Rudman & Dowd LLP.

ESG Research

Our ESG analysis is supported by MSCI ESG Research and Sustainalytics ratings.
We believe sustainable investing is crucial for long-term returns and risk management.
